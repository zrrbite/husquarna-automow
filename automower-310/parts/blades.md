# Blades — Automower 310

Razor-style pivoting blades on the cutting disc. Always replace as a full set (3 blades + 3 screws) so the disc stays balanced.

## Part numbers

- **577 86 46-03** — genuine Husqvarna Classic Blades, 9-pack with screws (3 sets of 3)
- Third-party "long-life" 9-packs are widely available (e.g. Rasenfreund titan blades on Amazon); meaningfully cheaper but not Husqvarna-spec steel.

## Interval

- Inspect every 2–4 weeks in season
- Replace as a set every 1–2 months in heavy use, or when visibly worn / chafed / bent
